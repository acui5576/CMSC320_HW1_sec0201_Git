Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: To track changes over time, work on features safely with others, and have backup and recovery.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Retail and commerce, for example tracking customer behavior to better market products


Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Git merge combines branches by preserving full history. Git rebase moves a branch's commits to the tip of another, creating a linear history by rewriting commits.
